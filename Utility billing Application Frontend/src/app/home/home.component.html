BHARAT BIJILI CORPORATION

Bharat Bijili Corporation (BBC) is a Electric Power Utility Company in Pune which is a private distributor of power to local residents. BBC has 20 employees in their Back Office Operations team to manage the Electricity bill payments. The power supplied by BBC is used by 10,000 families in the local township.
